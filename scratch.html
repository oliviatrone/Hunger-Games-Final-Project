Top Facts About The Hunger Games

  1. The Hunger Games are a yearly event in Panem.
  2. Contestants fight until only one remains alive.
  3. The story takes place in a dystopian future.
  4. The protagonist, Katniss Everdeen, volunteers as tribute.
    • Katniss is from District 12.
    • She becomes the "Mockingjay" symbol of rebellion.
