Fun Facts about the Hunger Games

  1. Katniss Everdeen is known as the "Mockingjay".
    • She was the first female victor of the 74th Hunger Games.
    • Her weapon of choice is a bow and arrow.
  2. The Capitol is the center of power in Panem.
    • It is an advanced city with extravagant luxuries and technology.
    • The people of the Capitol live in stark contrast to those in the districts.
  3. The Hunger Games is an annual event held by the Capitol.
